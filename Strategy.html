Local Strategy

  • Mental Health
  • Homelessness
  • Social Order

Case Management    


We have several ongoing services that we provide to those with mental health issues.

Benefit Claims


Many of those with mental health challenges are qualified for public assistance programs but just need help applying. 

Substance Abuse Treatment


Every journey begins with just one step. 

Profiles

We keep confidential profiles containing information on the people we serve in order to follow up and and make sure they are getting the assistance needed. 

Information Packets

Every person in need who we encounter gets a resource directory , information about our services, and a map to other services offered in the community 

Resumes / Job Training

One way we help people get back on their feet is by guiding them through the local career center. There we help them search for jobs, prepare resumes, and we provivd interview coaching. 

Sharing Knowledge


 

One way we help share knowedge is when we help members of law enforcement better understand the conditions  of those who suffer from mental illness. We also share this information with incarcerated inmates. This is for the purpose of promoting protection and safety for everyone. 

Partnerships


Our country draws its strength from unity. We encourage that unity by helping local companies become more involved in charitable causes. We also organize members of the community so that they can contribute toward efforts to make the community more healthy, happy, and safe.

Research



Information gathering and study of that information allows us to provide qualified assistance to government administrators. Some of the areas we are currenly working on is public assistance fraud, misuse of public funds, officer involved shootings, deaths in custody,  business consumer  complaints, and more.
