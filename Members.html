Directors

Michael R. Kitterman

PRESIDENT

Michael lives in Northen California where he has been writing data driven software applications since high school. Michael has recently turned more of his attention toward what he describes as, "Solutions that work toward eliminating our society's ongoing failure to protect one another from preventable death." 

Paula M. Martin

SECRETARY

Paula lives in the San Francisco Bay Area where her and her husband of 17 years have raised three exceptional children. Paula has had a successful career in real estate, and now also acts as co-director and secretary for American Citizens Institute. When asked why she volunteers so much of her time at A.C.I. she replied,  "Nothing is more rewarding than to know that your work and effort is saving lives."
